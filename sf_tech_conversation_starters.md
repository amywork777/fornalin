# San Francisco Tech Conversation Starters

## Tech History Facts to Share During the Date

### Early Tech Origins
- The transistor radio was invented in the Bay Area, helping launch the region's tech reputation
- Silicon Valley got its name from the silicon chips manufactured in the area starting in the 1950s
- The Homebrew Computer Club, where Apple's first computers were showcased, met in Menlo Park just south of San Francisco

### San Francisco Tech Landmarks
- The Transamerica Pyramid was one of the first buildings to use a computer-assisted design process
- South Park in SoMa was the center of the first dot-com boom in the 1990s
- The Palace of Fine Arts originally housed technology exhibits for the 1915 World's Fair

### Modern Tech Culture
- Many successful startups like Airbnb, Twitter, and Uber were founded in San Francisco
- The Mission District transformed from a working-class neighborhood to a tech hub in the 2010s
- The "Painted Ladies" Victorian houses have been featured in countless tech company advertisements and promotional materials
