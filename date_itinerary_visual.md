# San Francisco Date Itinerary Visual Concept

This document describes a visual itinerary map that will be featured on the capybara-themed date invitation website.

## Visual Style
- **Color Scheme:** Purple gradient background (ranging from light lavender to deep purple)
- **Accent Colors:** Gold/yellow accents (for capybara illustrations) and green accents (for hummus elements)
- **Typography:** Playful, rounded font for headings; clean, modern sans-serif font for descriptions

## Map Layout
The visual will be designed as an illustrated map of San Francisco with our date stops highlighted along a purple path. Each stop will feature:

1. **San Francisco Zoo & Gardens**
   - Illustrated Element: Cute capybara illustration with heart eyes
   - Timing Badge: "9:30 AM - Start our adventure!"
   - Mini Description: "Meet the capybaras of SF Zoo"

2. **Ritual Coffee Roasters**
   - Illustrated Element: Coffee cup with purple steam and tiny capybara peeking over rim
   - Timing Badge: "11:30 AM - Coffee break"
   - Mini Description: "Specialty coffee & hummus plates"

3. **California Academy of Sciences**
   - Illustrated Element: Scientist capybara with microscope against purple galaxy background
   - Timing Badge: "1:00 PM - Science exploration"
   - Mini Description: "Tech wonders & purple bioluminescence"

4. **Yerba Buena Gardens**
   - Illustrated Element: Capybara relaxing among purple flowers
   - Timing Badge: "3:30 PM - Garden picnic"
   - Mini Description: "Purple beet hummus & art"

5. **Truly Mediterranean/Old Jerusalem**
   - Illustrated Element: Capybara wearing a chef's hat with a hummus platter
   - Timing Badge: "6:00 PM - Dinner time"
   - Mini Description: "The best hummus in SF!"

6. **Urban Putt**
   - Illustrated Element: Capybara with golf club against purple lighting
   - Timing Badge: "8:00 PM - Indoor mini-golf"
   - Mini Description: "Tech-designed mini-golf course"

7. **The View Lounge**
   - Illustrated Element: Capybara in bowtie holding purple cocktail with city view
   - Timing Badge: "10:00 PM - Skyline views"
   - Mini Description: "Purple cocktails & SF lights"

## Interactive Elements
When implemented on the website:
- Hovering over each stop will enlarge the illustration and reveal more details about the activity
- A "full itinerary" button will expand to show the complete date plan
- Subtle animations will show tiny capybaras moving along the path between locations

## Special Features
- A "personalized for Nalin" banner in purple at the top
- A subtle "Will you join me?" message that appears at the end of the path
- RSVP buttons styled as cute capybara faces (one happy for "yes," one sad for "no")
- Background music option with gentle, whimsical tunes that match the mood of the adventure

This visual itinerary will serve as the centerpiece of the website design, providing an engaging and memorable way to present the date invitation.
